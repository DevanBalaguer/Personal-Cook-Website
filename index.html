Who is Ulva?

While the profession is not that uncommon, not all cooks are cut out for a life of adventure. Those who decide to take on the evils of the world are brave, resourceful, and adaptable. This kind of cook needs to keep up with the noblest of knights, the cunningest of thieves, and the wisest of wizards, all while carrying a set of heavy pots and pans. On top of the day’s questing needs, a cook needs the extra energy at the end of the day to work over the heat of an improvised kitchen.




Not only does the Ulva work afterhours, but she too works during the adventuring day. Ulva is a cunning and resourceful cook, utilizing her downtime to find ingredients in order to prepare meals for your adventuring day. She pours her heart and soul into preparing incredible meals that refresh the body and warm the spirit. This adventuring cook’s spirit and work ethic are unmatched.
